# Ability Checks
To make an ability check, roll 1d20 and add the relevant ability modifier.
Compare the total to a Difficulty Class (DC): 10 easy, 15 medium, 20 hard.

# Attack Rolls
Roll 1d20 + ability modifier + proficiency. If the total meets or exceeds the
target's Armor Class (AC), the attack hits. On a hit, roll the weapon's damage.

# Advantage / Disadvantage
Roll 2d20 and take the higher (advantage) or lower (disadvantage) result.

# Setting
This is a medieval fantasy world. There is no modern or sci-fi technology.
